About Ivan

As a self-taught audio engineer, I picked up a relentless work ethic and ability to efficiently troubleshoot under strict time constraints. This skill combined with day-to-day production management and working side by side with our clients (artists) got me to the peak of my industry, touring with multiple acts around the country. Although I'm new to the field, I'm more than confident in my ability to pick up new skills and adapt to an ever-growing work enviroment in software development.

I'm eager to get my foot in the door and learn from everyone around me. Doing so in my last career taught me a lot about learning in an objective sense. I realized how achievable growth is when matched with dedication, eagerness, and open-mindedness. As a philosophy student in college, I understand the importance of the bigger picture, and being mindful of your work environment (when and when not to ask questions, be helpful, etc.). I believe, with the culmination of my past experiences, I can successfully fit right in on any team and create great work.

My skill sets in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, node.JS, and React, matched with my efficient troubleshooting ability and hunger to learn, will make me an asset anywhere.

Outside of development, I enjoy working on/riding my motorcycle and playing guitar/listening to music!
